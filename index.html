My Approach

Collaborative

I believe in the power of collaboration to adapt to your needs. As an extension of your team, I immerse myself in your vision and goals. Coming from a diverse background in India and working across the globe in Europe and Scandinavia for over 10 years, I thrive in cross-cultural communication.
