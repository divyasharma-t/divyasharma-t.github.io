My Approach

Collaborative

I believe in the power of collaboration to adapt to your needs. As an extension of your team, I immerse myself in your vision and goals. From understanding your daily struggles to giving you tools to navigate successfully in your country, to collaborating with you on a speaking request, I work closely with you to develop an experience that not only reflects your brand but exceeds your expectations.

Cross Cultural

Coming from a diverse background in India and working across the globe in Europe and Scandinavia for over 10 years, I thrive in cross-cultural communication. In any part of our daily work, we need interaction with other cultures. My approach is consultative, personal and professional here with my clients.
